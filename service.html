SERVICE

Minimal Maintenance

Regular maintenance is not required. Carson vehicles are designed to eliminate the need for service. With remote diagnostics and over-the-air software updates, you'll spend less time in the shop and more time driving.

Maximum Convenience

Schedule service, track repairs and message directly with your Service Center at any time.

Mobile Service Anywhere

If your car requires attention, Carson mobile technicians can complete most repairs wherever you park. Even if they can't fix your issue right away, Carson Service Centers can identify and diagnose problems before you arrive for faster service.

Roadside Emergency Service

In an emergency, Roadside Assistance is available when you need it most. We are available 24/7 and 365 days a year to help with roadside emergencies.

Repairs

We offer a full range of collision repair services at our Carson-Approved Collision Centers, Carson Service Centers, and Carson Collision Centers.

Scuffs and Dent

Fix minor dents, scuffs and more at your local Carson Service Center. Faster service is possible with an optimized service flow.
