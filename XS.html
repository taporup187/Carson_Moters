Summary of XS luxury

TCarson Motors offers 1 Electric Engine. The automatic transmission is available on this model. Five seats are available in the car, which measures 3993mm in length, 1811mm in width and 2498mm in wheelbase. The price range of Carson Motors is between Rs. 15.85 lakh and Rs. 25.87 lakh. A 30.2kWh battery powers the front mounted electric motor. Carson's internal testing indicates that the battery's range will be over 300km on a single charge. The Carson motors can be charged using either a regular 15A charging socket or a CCS 2 fast charger. In just one hour, the fast charger will charge an electric vehicle from zero to 80 percent, while the 15A socket will charge it from 20 percent to 100 percent in about eight hours.
